Hey! I'm Keegan,

I was born and raised in South Africa,
where my childhood was spent being dragged to remote places in Africa by my parents.
This is where it started, the spark of trying to capture a beauty that few people got to witness.

Fast forward a few years and I’ve moved across the pond to London, because, why not?
And I might’ve had a dream to make it in the big city or something like that.

I’ve transitioned more into portraiture and travel photography from wildlife photography.
My goal is to expose a raw realness of everyday people that isn’t seen much anymore.
I shoot on a mix between 35mm film and a modern mirrorless camera to challenge my creativity.
